The Motoplat brand stands for high quality test equipment. Premium European quality, modern technology and easy to use are the hallmarks of each and every Motoplat tester. The old website didn't have these standards. My job was to give the website a new life. The look and feel of the website had to be the same as the products they sell.

The trickiest part of this assignment was the different languages. A sentence in Dutch can be twice as long in Chinese or vice versa. It was quite a puzzle to get everything in place so that all languages came out well without the website being different for another language. With Jquery (the first and last time I used it) I created some small interactions.
