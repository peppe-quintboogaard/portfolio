This is a concept that I have in my head for several months and that I work on from time to time. I am a big fan of music, especially house music. I often search the internet to find songs and usually these songs have not been released. This application is a personal database where you can store these tracks with the corresponding source and release date. When the date is that the song will be released, the user will be notified.

It is a server side built application that runs on Node.js and Express. A MongoDb database is linked where all data is stored. In the future I want to expand the concept, where users can share their data among themselves, making it a kind of music platform.
